Kurt Cobain

Born in Aberdeen on February 20, 1967, Kurt Cobain was an American singer, musician, and songwriter who formed the band Nirvana with Krist Novoselic and Aaron Burckhard in 1987. They established as part of the Seattle music scene which later became known as grunge. Cobain, who died in 1994, has been remembered as one of the most iconic rock musicians in the history of alternative music.
